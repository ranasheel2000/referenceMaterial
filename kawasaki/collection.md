## Burnable Garbage (普通ごみ) – Kawasaki (Inadazutsumi, Tama Ward)

**Collection Days:** Monday & Thursday  
**Put out by:** 8:00 AM on the collection day  
*Do not put it out the night before unless your building specifically allows it.*

---

## 🔥 What Goes in Burnable Garbage

### 🍱 Kitchen Waste
- Food scraps, leftovers  
- Eggshells, bones  
- Used tea leaves / coffee grounds  
- Small amounts of cooking oil absorbed in paper  

> Drain water well before throwing away.

---

### 🧻 Dirty Paper / Non-Recyclable Paper
- Tissues, paper towels  
- Dirty paper packaging  
- Receipts (thermal paper)  
- Paper cups with plastic coating  
- Greasy pizza boxes  

---

### 🧴 Plastics WITHOUT the “プラ” Mark
- Plastic items that are **not** containers/packaging  
- Toothbrushes  
- CD/DVD cases  
- Small plastic household goods  
- Broken plastic hangers  

---

### 👕 Small Household Items
- Clothes (if not recyclable)  
- Small leather goods  
- Rubber items  
- Small stuffed toys  
- Small bags  

---

### 🩹 Hygiene Items
- Diapers (remove solid waste first)  
- Sanitary products  
- Bandages  

---

## 🚫 What Does NOT Go in Burnable Garbage

- PET bottles → Recycling day  
- Cans / bottles → Recycling day  
- Cardboard / milk cartons → Paper recycling day  
- Plastic packaging with プラ mark → Plastic recycling day  
- Batteries → Separate recycling day  
- Large items → Requires bulky garbage reservation  

---

## 🛍 How to Dispose
- Use transparent or semi-transparent bags  
- Tie the bag securely  
- Place at your designated collection point
